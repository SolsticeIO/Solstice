# Required Website Pages

Based on the URLs currently configured in the Solstice app, you need to create the following pages on your website (`solsticeio.vercel.app`):

1. **Home Page**
   - URL: `https://solsticeio.vercel.app/`
   - Linked from: About Screen (Website link)

2. **Donate Page**
   - URL: `https://solsticeio.vercel.app/donate`
   - Linked from: About Screen, Support Dialog (Star icon), and Onboarding Community Screen

3. **Privacy Policy Page**
   - URL: `https://solsticeio.vercel.app/privacy`
   - Linked from: About Screen (Privacy Policy link)
